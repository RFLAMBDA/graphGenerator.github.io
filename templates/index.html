Step 4: Gain & Pout Shift (Final Adjustments)

?

What this step does:

  • Apply final adjustments to your gain and output power curves
  • Fine-tune the positioning of your frequency curves
  • Generate the final optimized plot
  • Download your processed data and plot

Parameters:

  • Gain Shift: Optional comma-separated values to adjust gain curves (e.g., "0.5,-0.3,0")
  • Pout Shift: Optional comma-separated values to adjust output power curves (e.g., "1,0,-2")

How shifts work:

  • Positive values move curves up/right
  • Negative values move curves down/left
  • Values correspond to your frequency order
  • Leave the value "0" for no adjustment

Final Output:

  • Processed plot with all adjustments applied
  • Clean numeric data for further analysis
  • Ready for publication or analysis
